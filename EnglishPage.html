English

English is one of the most spoken languages on the planet and a lot of people believe it to be an essential skill to have in life. Here you can gain a better understanding of english and its uses!

In english, words are put into seperate catagories like nouns and verbs. Nouns are words that are the names of places, people or things. A proper noun is a word that uses a capital letter at the start of the word. For example, Adam, France.

Verbs are action words like run or walk. They are used to describe an action. When verbs and nouns are put together, they form a sentence. For example, Adam walks home every day.
