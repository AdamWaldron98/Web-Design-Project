About us!

Here at Eduk8, our aim is to educate students through the use of accessible technology.

Since 2007, our team has been working with communties all over Kenya with the aim of educating the population. You are able to view this website thanks to the wonders of technology! This computer was donated to your community by our organisation with the aim of providing you with knowledge about History, Geography, Maths and English

You can click on any of the links to learn about one of the topics! Once you are on a page, you can go to another one by clicking any of the topic names, these are called links. They let you to move from one page to another; try one!
