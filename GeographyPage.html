Geography

The capital of Kenya is Nairobi. Kenya is made up of low areas that rise into highlands. Kenya borders with several other countries such as Ethiopia, Somalia and Uganda. The climate in Kenya varies all over the county, ranging from cool every day to always hot and sunny. This depends on how far away from the sea the location is.

There are several natural resources to be found in Kenya such as limestone, salt, oil and gas. The highest mountain in Kenya is "Mount Kenya"! It stands at 5,199 meters above sea level.

The largest lake in Kenya is Lake Turkana, it is over 280 kilometers long and 32 kilometers wide.
